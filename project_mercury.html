Echoes of Unrest is an experimental tactical RPG where all your decisions branch out the story. And sometimes in really drastic ways!

In Echoes of Unrest your choices matter!

I will add more info as I make more progress. In the meantime here's a really early video of the game:


Echoes of Unrest is a sci-fi story set in a fictional world with a rich and vast lore spaning thousands of years.

By design the game will not be terribly long, but it will be wide; allowing you to explore multiple branching story paths as you make your way through the story.
