[Project VULCAN]

- ACTUAL NAME TBD -

Genre: Turn-Based Tactics

Inspired by Advances Wars with the combat mechanics of a modern CRPG! Gridless movement and trajectory based abilities.

Manage your army and structures, and position your squads to outsmart your opponent.

Vulcan is played Online Multiplayer, Coop and PvP. And will probably release as Early Access in 2025, with a playable demo before that.

This game will have 2 main game-modes: "War Scenarios" and "Skirmishes".

  • WarScenarios: 1-4 players Coop PvE. Semi-procedural missions with objectives.
  • Skirmishes: 2-4 players PvP. Classic battles between factions.

This game is actively in PreProduction mid its prototype phase. So A LOT is going to change before Production begins in a few months.
