A Little Age

Build and expand a network of settlements, each with its own little economy. Manage the flow of resources between them at your own pace, and watch a civilization emerge in this minimalist settlement builder.




A Little Age is a cozy little city builder where you start with a single hamlet, reveal the world as your civilization expands, establish new settlements, and grow them into a connected network of towns and cities.

EACH SETTLEMENT HAS ITS OWN LITTLE ECONOMY

Each settlement has its own production and local economy. Hire delivery guys to move resources between settlements, boost new towns, and support places that have to grow in less-than-ideal conditions.


A NEW UNIQUE MAP EVERY TIME YOU PLAY

Each time you play the world is unique. Biomes and resources create new challenges and problems to solve, and not every settlement will have an ideal environment. Some settlements will depend on the trade network to function properly. A Little Age uses a detailed procedural world generation system to create a new world every time.


ALL SETTLEMENTS WORK TOGETHER

As your civilization grows, you will unlock new technological upgrades and build a great temple to celebrate your new homeland. Your settlements can support each other, pool their resources, and grow into a healthier civilization through collaboration.
