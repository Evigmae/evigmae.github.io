"WarKings, M.D.W.S."

Turn-Based-Tactics

Sort of Advanced Wars with turn-based CRPG combat; gridless movement, and trajectory based abilities.

Manage your army and resources, and make tactical decisions on the battlefield.

WarKings has 2 game modes:

- Thes main one is "War Scenarios"; 1-4p Coop PvE. You and your allies wage a campaign with objectives against an enemy faction. Each time the world and objectives are different!

- Second game mode is "Battles": 2-4 players PvP. Classic battles between factions. When the goal is to be the last faction standing.

All game modes can be played Online. And/or you can add RoboPlayers to any game mode to fill player seats.


WarKings is planned to first release in Early Access on PC. (ETA Q4 2026)
