Asterminer

Genre: Exploration, Management

CodeName: Project Ceres



Asterminer is the spiritual sequel of the Asterminer that released on Android/iOS in 2016. It has a much larger scope though; serene, atmospheric journey in space. Control a mining ship, train, and operator on distant planets and asteroids.
Gather resources for your loved ones back home in this immersive, slow-paced, grounded sci-fi adventure.


In Asterminer, explore the calm of space in as you look for the resources you need to build up your science colony. Operate a mining ship, a train, or walk around as the operator across distant planets and asteroids in a grounded sci-fi setting. It's a casual yet deep adventure with no danger, no enemies, and no violence, making space exploration a beautiful, contemplative experience. Enjoy the awesome music and the joy of gathering resources for your loved ones in this serene, unique game that celebrates the wonders of the cosmos.
