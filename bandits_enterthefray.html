[BANDITS: ENTER THE FRAY]

This game is a reboot of "Bandits", a game I did before I really knew what I was doing.
So long story short, "BANDITS: ENTER THE FRAY" is a new game, sort of a remake, but also a reimagining!

I'm taking the time to do everything right. And make this game the best it can be. With a lengthy Prototype and later PreAlpha phases.

Prototype is UE4, but final game is going to be UE5.

[About BANDITS: ENTER THE FRAY]

"Bandits: Enter the Fray" is an online multiplayer 3rd person vehicular combat shooter. It features incredible fully destructible environments, and many characters with unique play-styles and abilities.

Planned features:

  • Play with any of the many cool characters in all game modes!

  • Each character has their own unique play-style and abilities!

  • All environments are quite completely destructible. Even the terrain itself!

  • Play the PvE Campaign solo, 2 players split screen, or 2-4 players Online!

  • Complete challenging procedural and replayable PvE Missions 2-4 players Online!

  • Fight in the scorching PvP Battle Arenas with other players in teams or free for all! (Max players TBD)

"BANDITS: ENTER THE FRAY" will release on 2023 for PC, XBOX, PLAYSTATION and SWITCH.
