[BANDITS: ENTER THE FRAY]

BANDITS: Enter the Fray is a sequel/remake of the previus Bandits game I did before I really knew what I was doing. Made possible by the EPIC MEGA GRANT awarded by EPIC GAMES in 2020.

Originally I had planned to rework the older version that's released on STEAM. But after much work and consideration I opted to redo the whole thing. This game is Super Smash Bross Melee to Super Smash Bros.
So long story short, BANDITS: ENTER THE FRAY is going to be a new game, sort of a remake, but also a reimagining!

I'm taking the time to re-do everything completely from scratch. I've learned so much in the last few years, and I wish to bring all of that to the table. Everything is going to look cooler and feel better!

BANDITS: ENTER THE FRAY has been in pre-production since January 2020. So I'll start sharing more about it as soon as I have stuff that reflects the level if awesomeness I'm aiming for!
Be sure to join my mailing list to get news when something exciting happens!

[About BANDITS: ENTER THE FRAY]

"Bandits: Enter the Fray" is an online multiplayer 3rd person mech tactical shooter. It features incredible fully destructible environments, and lots of characters with unique play-styles and abilities.

Is powered by UE5.

Planned features:

  • Play with any of the many cool characters!

  • Each character has their own unique play-style and abilities!

  • All environments are quite completely destructible!

  • Play the Story Campaign Mode solo or 2-4 players Coop!

  • Compete in the scorching Battle arenas several players in teams or free for all! (Max players TBD)

  • Bandits is going to be playable online or on a single machine!

"Bandits: Enter the Fray" is intended to be released in 2022!
