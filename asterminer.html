*Game is currently under development. Nov 2023

In Asterminer, explore the calm of space in a relaxed, atmospheric journey. Operate a mining ship, train, or walk around as the operator across distant planets and asteroids in a grounded sci-fi setting. It's a casual yet deep adventure with no danger, no enemies, and no violence, making space exploration a beautiful, contemplative experience. Enjoy the awesome music and the joy of gathering resources for your loved ones in this serene, unique game that celebrates the wonders of the cosmos.

More information will come soon as I make more progress with its development.
