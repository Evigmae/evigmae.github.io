[ASTERMINER]

Asterminer is currently retired from all platforms. It released for Android and iOS in 2016.


It is a relaxed exploration adventure with some puzzle elements. You went through levels finding crystals to fill your train with, and continued doing so utill you did all the levels.
Asterminer has 5 World each with its own environment style, and each world had 10 levels.

What the future holds!

In 2021 I started working on a spiritual sequel of the same name, but for PC/Consoles: Project Ceres! It will be the next iteration of Asterminer!


Thanks! And see you on the next adventure!
