BANDITS

Bandits is an indie couch-multiplayer. A modern yet classic Tank Battle Arena game. It has fully destructible environments, and 8 characters with unique play-styles and abilities.

  • Play with any of the 8 awesome characters!

  • Each character has their own play-style and abilities!

  • Bandits has fully destructible environments!

  • Game modes are "The Story Campaign", and "The Battle Arena".
