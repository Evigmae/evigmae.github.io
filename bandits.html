[Working on Bandits 2.0: Enter the Fray!]

Bandits has been awarded an Epic Mega Grant by the amazing Epic Games.

Originally I had planned to rework the version that's released on Steam. But after much work and consideration it started to feel wrong to just erase the v1.
So long story short, Bandits 2.0 is going to be a new game, sort of a remake, but also its own new thing!

I'm taking the time to re-do everything, completely from scratch. I've learned so much in the last couple of years, and I want to bring all of that to the table. Everything is going to be cooler and more alive!

The game has been in pre-production/pre-alpha since January 2020. And I'll start sharing more of the game as soon as I have stuff that reflects the level if awesomeness I'm aiming for!

[About Bandits 2.0: Enter The Fray]

Bandits is a shared-screen multiplayer game. A modern -yet classic- Tank Battle Arena! It will have awesome fully destructible environments, and 8 characters with unique play-styles and abilities.

It's meant to homage beloved classics such as Star Fox 64 and Contra III. Not so much for the gameplay, but for the scope and feel of those games, which to this day keep influencing my game-making choices!

Planned features:

  • Play with any of the 8 main characters!

  • Each character has their own unique play-style and abilities!

  • All environments are quite completely destructible.

  • Play the Story Mode solo or 2 players Coop!

  • Compete in the Battle arena 2-4 players in teams or free for all!

  • Bandits is going to be playable online Cross-Platform multiplayer!

Bandits 2.0: Enter the Fray is intended to be completed around mid 2021!
